## Full Description

### Headline
**SilicIA: Search & Chat with Zero Carbon Footprint**

### Main Description
SilicIA is a privacy-first search and chat assistant designed for environmentally conscious users who demand data security. By running entirely on your device with Apple's efficient on-device AI, SilicIA eliminates the environmental cost of cloud processing while keeping your data completely private.

### Why SilicIA?

**🌍 Reduce Your Carbon Footprint**
- On-device AI processing eliminates energy-intensive cloud servers
- Optimized for Apple Silicon for maximum efficiency
- No data sent to remote servers = lower bandwidth = lower emissions
- Every search keeps your carbon footprint local

**🔒 Complete Privacy, Complete Control**
- No tracking, no data collection, no third-party analytics
- All conversations stored locally on your device
- Your PDFs, your context, your data—never uploaded anywhere
- See exactly what's analyzed and how

**🔍 Search Your Way**
- Privacy-respecting DuckDuckGo integration for web search
- Get AI summaries without sacrificing your privacy
- Context-aware results powered by your own documents

**💬 Chat Offline**
- Ask questions about your PDFs without internet
- Attach URLs for web context
- Build conversations with full chat history
- Perfect for reading papers, analyzing documents, research

### Key Features

✨ **Dual Search Modes**
- **Fast Mode**: Get quick answers with DuckDuckGo + on-device AI
- **Deep Mode**: Extensive research with more web results
- **Offline Mode**: Chat with just the model—no internet needed

📎 **Context-Aware Intelligence**
- Drag-and-drop PDF support
- Add URLs for web context
- Persistent chat history with conversation management
- Retrieve answers from your own documents

⚡ **Optimized for Impact**
- Tuned for Apple Silicon
- Efficient power management—won't drain your battery
- Fast responses without heavy cloud dependencies

🌐 **Your Data, Your Rules**
- Conversations saved locally in SwiftData
- No cloud synchronization, no accounts needed
- Delete your history anytime—complete control

### Perfect For
- Researchers analyzing papers
- Students reviewing documents
- Professionals researching topics privately
- Anyone concerned about digital privacy and environmental impact

### Technical Details
- **Requirements**: macOS 15+, Apple Silicon
- **AI**: Local on-device processing with Apple's Foundation Models
- **Search**: Privacy-respecting DuckDuckGo integration
- **Storage**: Local SwiftData persistence
- **No trackers, no ads, no subscriptions**
